Est.

Company Founded

PHARMA-Connectiv Pte Ltd established in Singapore with a mission to bridge global health innovation and regional healthcare needs.

Phase 1

Pometate Launch

Launched Pometate — our flagship vegetarian dietary supplement featuring pomegranate, green tea, turmeric and broccoli extracts. FDA registered, made in USA.

Phase 2

ProstaMax Introduction

Expanded into men's health with ProstaMax — a clinically formulated prostate health supplement combining Saw Palmetto, Selenium, Zinc and Pumpkin Seed.

Phase 3

HP Cell Vitaran J Partnership

Partnered with BR PHARM (Korea) to distribute the world's first clinically-trialed Polynucleotide intra-articular injection — Korea FDA and CE Class III approved.
