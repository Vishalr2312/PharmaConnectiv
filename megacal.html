What is MegaCal K2?

Comprehensive Bone Health
in One Tablet

MegaCal K2 is a nutraceutical film-coated tablet delivering a powerful combination of nutrients specifically formulated to support bone density, muscle function, cardiovascular health and energy production.

The unique inclusion of Vitamin K2 is key — it activates proteins that direct calcium specifically into the bones and teeth, while preventing it from accumulating in arteries and soft tissues. This makes MegaCal K2 superior to conventional calcium supplements.

Store in a cool & dry place, protect from direct sunlight. Usage as directed by the dietician or health practitioner. Nutraceutical, not for medicinal use.

Active Ingredients

Four Pillars of
Bone Health

Each ingredient in MegaCal K2 plays a distinct and complementary role in supporting overall bone and cardiovascular health.

🦴
Calcium Citrate · 1000mg
Calcium Citrate

Calcium Citrate is the most bioavailable form of calcium supplement. Unlike calcium carbonate, it can be absorbed without food and is particularly beneficial for those with low stomach acid.

Helps address low calcium levels and supports bone density
Superior absorption compared to calcium carbonate
Supports teeth mineralisation and muscle contraction
🩸
Vitamin K2-7 (1%) · 100mcg
Vitamin K2

Vitamin K2 (as Menaquinone-7) activates osteocalcin — the protein that anchors calcium into bone tissue. It also activates Matrix Gla Protein (MGP), which prevents arterial calcification.

Directs calcium into bones rather than arteries
Prevents damage to blood vessels from calcium deposits
Synergistic effect with Vitamin D3 for optimal calcium use
☀️
Vitamin D3 · 1000 IU
Vitamin D3

Vitamin D3 (cholecalciferol) is essential for calcium absorption from the intestines. Without adequate D3, even high calcium intake cannot be effectively used by the body for bone formation.

Treats and prevents bone disorders including osteoporosis
Enhances intestinal absorption of calcium and phosphorus
Supports immune system, muscle function and general well-being
⚡
Magnesium Hydroxide · 100mg + Zinc + Boron
Magnesium, Zinc & Boron

Magnesium supports over 300 enzymatic reactions including muscle and energy production. Zinc (3mg elemental) is essential for bone metabolism, and Boron (3mg) aids calcium and magnesium retention.

Magnesium supports muscle function and energy production
Zinc is essential for bone cell formation and immune health
Boron enhances the retention and use of calcium and magnesium
