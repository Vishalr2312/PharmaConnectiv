What is HP Cell Vitaran J?

Tissue Restoration
Through Science

HP Cell VITARAN J is a biomaterial for tissue restoration using Polynucleotide (PN) that can be treated to reduce physical friction between the articular cartilage of the synovial (joint). It helps improve the functional mobility of the knee and relieves joint pain and inflammation.

Polynucleotide is a long-chain DNA polymer extracted from salmon or trout. It is injected into the joint cavity to reduce mechanical friction of the joint by maintaining its volume for a certain period of time — offering sustained relief.

Key Technical Facts
→Long-chain DNA polymer extracted from salmon or trout
→Viscoelastic properties similar to Hyaluronic Acid (HA)
→Forms a 3D gel structure providing cushioning and mechanical support
→Reduces inflammation through natural biological mechanisms
How It Works

Three Mechanisms
of Action

HP Cell VITARAN J works through three distinct, synergistic biological mechanisms to restore joint function and relieve pain.

🧬
High Molecular DNA Polymer

PN features a polymerized DNA structure with a highly viscous gel-like texture, similar to hyaluronic acid (HA), providing elasticity and mechanical support.

🦵
Enhances Functional Mobility

By forming a 3D gel structure, PN creates a cushioning effect that helps reduce physical friction within the joints, restoring natural movement.

💊
Reduces Inflammation & Pain

PN helps relieve joint pain and inflammation through its natural biological mechanisms, promoting improved joint comfort and daily function.
