From Turmeric · Anti-pigmentation
Curcumin

Curcumin is obtained from Turmeric. It decreases excess melanin production and lightens skin tone. It also aids in collagen deposition on the skin, supporting a firmer and more youthful appearance.

Decreases excess melanin production for a lighter complexion
Aids collagen deposition for firmer, younger-looking skin
Anti-inflammatory properties reduce skin redness and irritation

PhytoflORAL® · UV Protection + PhytoflORAL Carotenoids
Polypodium & PhytoflORAL®

Polypodium includes antioxidants that protect the skin from inflammatory diseases and UV damage. PhytoflORAL® contains phytoene and phytofluene — colorless carotenoids that lighten and level skin tone, aiding anti-aging and a more youthful appearance.

Immunoprotection against UVA and UVB radiation
Phytoene and phytofluene lighten and even skin texture
Anti-aging benefits for a more youthful skin appearance
