Gut Health · Oral Paste
Probioxin
Pro-Kolin Advanced for Dogs

A paste to support gut health, soothe the gut lining, and firm up faeces. Probioxin combines probiotics, prebiotics and gut flora stabilisers to restore healthy gut flora, aid in digestion, nutrient absorption and boost immunity by balancing the gut microbiome.

Active Composition
Probiotic: Enterococcus faecium (TFH–1904)
Prebiotic: Oligofructose (Beta-glucan) · Psyllium husk · Pectin
Gut Flora Stabiliser: Bentonite montmorillonite 170,000mg/kg
Technology additives: Acacia gum arabic · Kaolin
Excipients: Refined soyabean oil · Dextrose
ProbioticsPrebiotics Gut Flora BalanceOral Paste
Dosage by Dog Weight
Weight Volume Frequency
< 5kg 2ml Twice daily
5–15kg 3ml Twice daily
15–30kg 5ml Twice daily
> 30kg 7ml Twice daily

Give orally for as long as considered necessary or as advised by your veterinarian. Simultaneous administration of oral macrolides should be avoided. Store in a cool, dry place out of direct sunlight.

Anti-fungal · Shampoo
Kezopet
Ketoconazole, Chlorhexidine Gluconate & Cetrimide Shampoo

Kezopet is an anti-fungal shampoo providing comprehensive cover to companion animals. It is a triple-action combination of Ketoconazole, Chlorhexidine and Cetrimide — each contributing a distinct therapeutic role for complete skin and coat care.

Active Composition
Ketoconazole 2% w/v — broad-spectrum antifungal; controls flaking, scaling, itching
Chlorhexidine Gluconate 2% w/v — broad-spectrum bactericidal; kills fungi and yeast; cleanses skin; makes coat shiny and healthy
Cetrimide 0.1% w/v — antiseptic; kills bacteria and fungi; treats flaky and itchy scalp; useful in scabies and seborrheic dermatitis

Antibacterial · Antifungal Shampoo
Micohex
Medication Shampoo — Bacterial & Fungal Skin Infections

Micohex is a specialist medication shampoo for the treatment of common bacterial and fungal infections of the skin, and for the treatment of inflammatory forms of seborrhoea in companion animals.

Active Ingredients
Chlorhexidine gluconate 20g — broad-spectrum antibacterial and antifungal action
Miconazole nitrate 20g — effective antifungal; targets dermatophytes and yeasts
Aloe extract — soothes and conditions the skin; supports healing
