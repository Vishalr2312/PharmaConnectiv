Liposomal Technology

Why Liposomal?
Superior Absorption

Liposomal K2-7 (MK-7) and Liposomal D3 offer superior absorption and bioavailability compared to conventional supplements.

Liposomes — tiny fat-based spheres structurally similar to cell membranes — protect the vitamins from degradation by stomach acids and enzymes in the gastrointestinal tract. This ensures more of the active nutrient reaches the bloodstream intact.

Their structural similarity to cell membranes enables liposomes to fuse with human cells, directly delivering the encapsulated vitamins for optimal absorption and utilization. Liposomal formulations result in significantly higher plasma concentrations and improved vitamin status compared to traditional supplements.

Health Benefits

D3 & MK-7 Combination Benefits

Vitamin D3 and K2 work synergistically to support overall health across three key areas.

🦴
Bone Health

Vitamin D3 enhances calcium absorption from the digestive tract, while Vitamin K2 (MK-7) activates osteocalcin to direct calcium into bones — maintaining strong bones and reducing fracture risk.

❤️
Cardiovascular Health

Vitamin K2 activates Matrix Gla Protein (MGP), preventing calcium from accumulating in arteries and soft tissues — supporting arterial flexibility and cardiovascular health. D3 supports overall heart function.

🛡️
Immune & General Function

Vitamin D3 supports a healthy immune system and muscle function. The MK-7 form has a longer half-life in the body than other K forms — making it highly effective for sustained, prolonged benefits.
