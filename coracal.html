What is CORA-CAL 1000?

Three Nutrients,
One Complete Formula

CORA-CAL 1000 is a film-coated nutraceutical tablet formulated with three complementary nutrients — Calcium Citrate, Vitamin D3, and Vitamin C — each contributing a distinct and clinically recognised role in supporting bone health, calcium absorption, and connective tissue integrity.

The addition of Vitamin C sets CORA-CAL 1000 apart. Beyond its well-known antioxidant properties, Vitamin C is essential for collagen synthesis — the structural protein that forms the organic matrix of bone and supports the health of joints, tendons, and cartilage surrounding skeletal tissue.

Made under license from Jose Lea Pharma France, Lyon — each pack contains 3 × 10 film-coated tablets for convenient, continuous 24-hour bone support. Nutraceutical, not for medicinal use.

Key Mineral · 1000mg per tablet
Calcium Citrate

Calcium Citrate is among the most bioavailable forms of supplemental calcium. It can be absorbed with or without food and is particularly effective for individuals with low stomach acid, making it a superior choice over calcium carbonate.

Builds and maintains strong, dense bones and teeth
Effective absorption with or without food
Supports proper muscle contraction and nerve signalling
Absorption Booster · 1000 I.U. per tablet
Vitamin D3

Vitamin D3 (cholecalciferol) is the body's preferred form of Vitamin D. It regulates the expression of calcium-binding proteins in the intestinal lining, dramatically increasing the proportion of ingested calcium that enters the bloodstream.

Significantly enhances intestinal calcium absorption
Supports prevention and management of bone disorders
Contributes to immune and muscle function
Structural Support · 85mg per tablet
Vitamin C

Vitamin C (ascorbic acid) is an essential cofactor in collagen synthesis. Collagen forms the organic framework of bone and is the primary structural protein of cartilage, tendons, and the connective tissue surrounding every joint in the body.

Essential for collagen production and bone matrix formation
Supports joint, cartilage and connective tissue health
Powerful antioxidant protecting bone cells from oxidative stress
Mechanism of Action

How CORA-CAL 1000
Works Around the Clock

01
Calcium Supply

Calcium Citrate releases readily available calcium ions in the digestive tract, even in low-acid environments, providing a reliable and consistent supply of the body's most abundant mineral.

02
D3-Activated Uptake

Vitamin D3 triggers the production of calbindin and other calcium-transport proteins in the gut wall, increasing the fraction of calcium absorbed into the bloodstream by up to threefold compared to unsupplemented intake.

03
Collagen Scaffolding

Vitamin C activates prolyl hydroxylase and lysyl hydroxylase — enzymes required to cross-link collagen fibres into the resilient organic matrix onto which calcium is deposited to form strong, dense bone tissue.
